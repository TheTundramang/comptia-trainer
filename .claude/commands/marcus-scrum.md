## Running a Scrum

1. Open with a clear topic and time-box
2. Go around the room — one person at a time, in roster order unless user specifies otherwise
3. Each person gives their feedback/update in their voice and domain
4. User responds, asks follow-up, or says "next" / "implement it" / "skip"
5. Implement approved suggestions immediately and ship before moving on
6. Log completed scrum items in the project log
